Sigil

Sigil is a sustainable rave wear brand committed to quality rave gear and the culture and community it serves. In today's social landscape, raving importantly shifts the focus from the intensity of our world to a more present and fluid state of enjoyment, self-reflection, and letting go. While Sigil's mission is to catalyze change in ravers, offering a sustainable alternative to the fast fashion brands in the space, it also appeals to an aesthetic and functionally concerned target market.

  • About Me

    Hi, my name is Matthew Funk. I am a designer and illustrator who tries to connect deeply with people and the challenges they may face. I love problems with rigid boundaries; the smaller the box, the more I can think outside it. I love to express my personality and findings through creative endeavours, but I am first a listener. Listening fuels my growing understanding of the world and the people inside it.
