  • VPL

  • Focusing has never been harder. We encounter distractions at every turn. in cafes we end up listening to other people’s conversations. Working from home lends itself to doom scrolling and naps. Office work means guaranteed interruptions. People will go to any lengths to find focus. This campaign illustrates different kinds of focus and introduces the VPL as the place to do it no matter what you’re looking for.

  • Hi, my name is Matthew Funk. I am a Vancouver based designer and illustrator who likes connecting deeply with people. That helps me to solve their design challenges more effectively. Problems with tight constraints are catnip to me; the smaller the box, the more fun it is to think outside it.

  • My work starts with listening closely so I can really understand what people are trying to say. When I’m not doing that, I like to express myself creatively, usually by making something with my hands. If you need someone who listens closely, likes solving difficult problems, and isn’t afraid to get his hands dirty, I’m all ears.
