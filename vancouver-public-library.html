  • VPL

  • Focusing has never been harder. We encounter distractions at every turn. In cafes we end up listening to other people’s conversations. Working from home lends itself to doom scrolling and naps. Office work means guaranteed interruptions. People will go to any length to find focus. This campaign illustrates different kinds of focus and introduces the VPL as the place to do it no matter what you’re looking for.
